Presideltial Spa Pool Villa

A day that starts with breakfasting on a terrace with views out to sea, is always going to be a good one, so you can rest assured that every day that begins at the Presidential Spa Pool Villa on the Elounda complex is going to be very special.

This luxury villa, with swimming pool, spa and gym has been created with nothing less than ultimate relaxation in mind. Whether you’re lying on the sunbathing terrace or swimming against the current in the infinity pool, you’ll find you just breathe a little slower and deeper here – it’s impossible not to.

Inside the secluded property, rooms are decorated in a classically elegant style, with calming neutral shades and a Gustavian feel to the furniture that gives the villa a slightly regal feel. At the end of a busy day swimming, lounging and relaxing, you’ll sink into incredibly comfy beds in luxury en-suite bedrooms and sleep well knowing you’ll wake again to another sumptuous breakfast with a sea view.
