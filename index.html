Wellcome To Luxury Villas Elafonisi

Relax and enjoy your own private bit of paradise. These luxury beachfront villas in Crete boasts a unique location and views that will lift your heart each morning when you wake.
Elafonisi villas are here to offer a memorable stay for all those seeking a genuine, traditional island experience. Situated in the picturesque village of Livadia, just a short stroll from the village’s center, the villas are an incomparable, luxurious getaway, ideal for large families or group of friends wanting to experience privacy and serenity close to nature. The vast exterior of the villas welcome children to play, have fun, enjoy the sun and create the most unforgettable summer memories!
