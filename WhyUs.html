Why Choose Luxury Villas Elafonisi

The View

Direct oceanfront with views from every room, these spacious villas lives like your private home near the beach and offers unobstructed ocean views. Each villa has a panoramic terrace that is fully furnished for outdoor living. This is the perfect place to relax with a glass of chilled Prosecco while enjoying a memorable sea-side vacation.

The Luxury

These stylish villas features modern, elegant decor with a white and neutral color palette that reflects the current interior design trends. All finishes and furniture have been meticulously selected to create a classic, timeless design. The high-end quality of these villas is reflected in every detail and the services offered during your stay.

The Good Life

You can hire a private luxury yacht. This is the perfect choice for a luxurious day on the water.Among the many places where you can celebrate an event, a yacht is certainly the most original and unusual. A boat about 20 meters long with furnished cabins is like a floating house where you can accommodate all your friends and family for an event like no other.

The Night Life

In short distance you will find plenty of bars and clubs, some well-hidden in the picturesque alleys of the old town and others lining the water font with a gorgeous view to the night sky.For a more peaceful night, enjoy a long dinner in the many taverns around Crete. These taverns have delicious recipes and local wine or raki.

The Elafonisi Beach

Elafonisi is a magical place that consists of a small islet full of white and pink sandy beaches, which is very close to the main busy (during Summer) beach. You can get to the Elafonissi island on foot because the waters are shallow, sometimes without even getting one's toes wet, and therefore is ideal for families with small children.
