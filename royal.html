Royal Spa Pool Villa

This luxury villa in Crete has been a favourite with celebrities, politicians and royalty for some years, and it’s easy to see why.

Inside, the villa has been conceived and designed to the very highest of standards. All the en-suite bathrooms are marble, the bedrooms elegant and luxurious, from the soft furnishings to the sumptuous bed linens. The spacious living and dining rooms are sophisticated and welcoming – you’ll be more than happy to simply linger at the table after dinner, finishing the fine wines and looking out to sea as you chat to your fellow guests.

For a little added pampering, the villa has its very own spa, with gym, sauna and steam room – as well as the two Jacuzzis in the infinity pool outside. But all this aside, there’s nothing that improves one’s mind, body and soul like standing on that terrace in front of the sea, cocktail in hand, looking at one of the best sunsets you’ll ever see.
